This is a web app that provides an easy to analyse 3D plot of a special family of functions.
It is based on the math paper titled "A Polynomial With n Maxima and no Other Critical Points" by Ian Robertson.

The function f is defined as: $$ f = (yh^2 - 2x - 1)^2 + g $$ $$ h = \displaystyle \prod_{i=1}^{n - 1} (x - 2i) , n \geq 2 $$ $$ g = \int \displaystyle \prod_{i=1}^{2n - 1} (x - i) dx , n \geq 2 $$
